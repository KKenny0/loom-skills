Loom研究工坊

把散落的来源，织成能看出哪里站得住、哪里站不住的研究。

Weave scattered sources into research that shows where the foundations are solid and where they're not.

为什么 / Why

Problem

AI 研究一个主题时，会给出流畅、自信的回答。但来源之间有分歧，证据质量参差不齐，有些判断下面只是薄薄一层冰。分歧被抹平，弱主张和强证据听起来一样笃定。你得到一篇读起来很顺的文章，却看不出哪里地基是实的、哪里是虚的。

When AI researches a topic, it gives you a fluent, confident answer. But real sources disagree, evidence quality varies, and some claims rest on thin ice. Disagreements get smoothed over. Weak claims sound as certain as strong evidence. You get a readable article, but you can't tell which parts are solid and which aren't.

Loom

Loom 读的是你给的来源，不抹平冲突。它告诉你共识在哪、分歧在哪、哪些判断只靠弱证据撑着。三个月后回头看，每个判断仍然能追溯到出处。

Loom reads your sources and preserves the conflicts. It shows where sources agree, where they diverge, and which claims are only thinly supported. Three months later, every judgment still traces back to its source.
